Barcelona

Soak up the sun and vibrant energy of Barcelona, where Gaudi's architectural masterpieces meet Mediterranean beaches. Wander down La Rambla, savor delicious tapas, and experience the city's lively nightlife. Explore the stunning Sagrada Familia and Park Güell for a taste of artistic genius. End your day with a glass of sangria by the sea.

Milan

The fashion capital of Italy awaits with its blend of history, style, and sophistication. Marvel at the breathtaking Duomo, shop in the glamorous Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, and enjoy world-class Italian cuisine. Explore Leonardo da Vinci's Last Supper or take in the city's thriving arts scene. At night, sip an aperitivo in the trendy Navigli district.

Berlin

A city where history and modern creativity collide, Berlin is packed with culture, art, and legendary nightlife. Visit the Berlin Wall, Brandenburg Gate, and Museum Island for a deep dive into its fascinating past. Discover edgy street art, underground music scenes, and buzzing beer gardens. By night, Berlin's club scene offers endless possibilities.

Paris



The City of Light enchants with its timeless beauty, world-class cuisine, and romantic atmosphere. Stroll along the Seine, visit the Eiffel Tower, and explore the charming Montmartre district. Savor exquisite pastries at a Parisian café and shop along the Champs-Élysées. As the sun sets, enjoy a magical cruise on the river or a cabaret at the Moulin Rouge.
