Barcelona

Soak up the sun and vibrant energy of Barcelona, where Gaudi's architectural masterpieces meet Mediterranean beaches. Wander down La Rambla, savor delicious tapas, and experience the city's lively Culture. Explore the stunning Sagrada Familia and Park Güell for a taste of artistic genius.

Ireland, Scotland and Wales

Discover the Celtic spirit across Scotland, Wales, and Ireland — lands of legend, music, and untamed beauty. Wander through misty highlands, rolling green hills, and rugged coastlines steeped in history. Explore medieval castles, lively towns, and vibrant cultures where tradition meets warmth and charm. End your days with local fare, folk music, and the unforgettable hospitality of the Celtic isles.

Athene

Step into the cradle of civilization where ancient history meets modern Mediterranean flair. Gaze upon the majestic Acropolis, stroll through the vibrant Plaka district, and taste authentic Greek cuisine under the glow of the Parthenon. Explore world-class museums, bustling markets, and a thriving arts scene. As night falls, enjoy rooftop views in this timeless city of myths and marvels.

Paris



The City of Light enchants with its timeless beauty, world-class cuisine, and romantic atmosphere. Stroll along the Seine, visit the Eiffel Tower, and explore the charming Montmartre district. Savor exquisite pastries at a Parisian café and shop along the Champs-Élysées. As the sun sets, enjoy a magical cruise on the river or a cabaret at the Moulin Rouge.
